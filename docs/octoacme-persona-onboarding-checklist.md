## Purpose
Ensure new team members understand their role, responsibilities, and communication expectations when joining an OctoAcme project.

## How to Use This Template
1. Copy this checklist when onboarding a new team member
2. Customize based on the specific role and project context
3. Review with the new team member during their first week
4. Track completion and address any questions or gaps

---

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

### Project Context
- [ ] Review Project One-pager (problem statement, goals, success metrics)
- [ ] Understand project timeline and key milestones
- [ ] Review project charter and current status
- [ ] Access project repository and documentation
- [ ] Join project communication channels (Slack, Teams, email lists)

### Team Introductions
- [ ] Meet Project Manager and understand project coordination approach
- [ ] Meet Product Manager and understand product vision and priorities
- [ ] Meet other team members and understand their roles
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and decision-making processes

### Process & Artifacts
- [ ] Review OctoAcme project management overview
- [ ] Understand team rhythm (standups, syncs, demos, retrospectives)
- [ ] Access project board and understand workflow states
- [ ] Review Definition of Done and acceptance criteria standards
- [ ] Understand branching strategy and PR conventions (if applicable)

---

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### For Developers
- [ ] Review codebase structure and architecture documentation
- [ ] Set up local development environment
- [ ] Understand testing strategy (unit, integration, E2E)
- [ ] Review code review process and standards
- [ ] Understand CI/CD pipeline and deployment process
- [ ] Meet with DevOps Engineer to understand infrastructure and tooling
- [ ] Meet with QA/Testing to understand quality expectations
- [ ] Meet with UX Designer to understand design hand-off process (if applicable)
- [ ] Complete first small task or bug fix to familiarize with workflow

### For QA/Testing
- [ ] Review test strategy and test plan documentation
- [ ] Understand acceptance criteria format and validation process
- [ ] Access test environments and tools
- [ ] Review automated test suite and coverage goals
- [ ] Understand bug tracking and reporting process
- [ ] Meet with Developers to understand development workflow
- [ ] Meet with DevOps Engineer to understand CI/CD integration
- [ ] Meet with UX Designer to understand usability testing approach (if applicable)
- [ ] Shadow existing QA process for one sprint cycle

### For Product Managers
- [ ] Review product roadmap and backlog
- [ ] Understand user personas and customer segments
- [ ] Review success metrics and data sources
- [ ] Understand prioritization framework and decision criteria
- [ ] Meet with Stakeholder Group Leads to understand stakeholder landscape
- [ ] Meet with UX Designer to review research and design process
- [ ] Meet with Developers to understand technical constraints and opportunities
- [ ] Review historical product decisions and rationale

### For Project Managers
- [ ] Review project plan, timeline, and dependencies
- [ ] Access risk register and understand mitigation strategies
- [ ] Understand resource allocation and team capacity
- [ ] Review stakeholder communication plan and cadence
- [ ] Meet with Scrum Master to align on process facilitation (if applicable)
- [ ] Meet with DevOps Engineer to understand release process and constraints
- [ ] Meet with Stakeholder Group Leads to understand communication needs
- [ ] Review historical status reports and decision logs

### For Scrum Masters
- [ ] Understand team's agile maturity and current practices
- [ ] Review sprint cadence, velocity, and burndown trends
- [ ] Understand team composition and working agreements
- [ ] Access retrospective notes and action items
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to align on coordination approach
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand backlog management
- [ ] Identify current impediments and process improvement opportunities
- [ ] Facilitate first sprint ceremony (with shadowing if needed)

### For UX Designers
- [ ] Review design system and component library
- [ ] Understand user research findings and personas
- [ ] Review design tools and collaboration platforms
- [ ] Understand design review and approval process
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to align on user needs and priorities
- [ ] Meet with Developers to understand technical constraints
- [ ] Meet with QA/Testing to understand usability testing process
- [ ] Review accessibility standards and compliance requirements

### For DevOps Engineers
- [ ] Review infrastructure architecture and diagrams
- [ ] Access CI/CD pipeline configuration and documentation
- [ ] Understand deployment process and release cadence
- [ ] Review monitoring, alerting, and incident response procedures
- [ ] Access production systems and on-call rotation (if applicable)
- [ ] Meet with Developers to understand build and deployment needs
- [ ] Meet with QA/Testing to understand test environment requirements
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to align on release planning
- [ ] Review security scanning and compliance requirements

### For Stakeholder Group Leads
- [ ] Understand stakeholder group composition and interests
- [ ] Review communication plan and update cadence
- [ ] Understand feedback collection and prioritization process
- [ ] Access stakeholder communication channels and templates
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to align on status reporting
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand product vision and roadmap
- [ ] Identify key stakeholder concerns and expectations
- [ ] Review approval and decision-making processes

### For Stakeholders
- [ ] Review Project One-pager and understand project goals
- [ ] Understand your role in providing input and approvals
- [ ] Know your Stakeholder Group Lead (if applicable)
- [ ] Understand communication cadence (updates, reviews, demos)
- [ ] Know how to provide feedback and escalate concerns
- [ ] Review milestone schedule and expected decision points
- [ ] Understand success metrics and how they will be measured

---

## Communication Expectations

### Daily/Weekly Rhythms
- [ ] Understand which meetings/standups you are expected to attend
- [ ] Know how to report blockers and request help
- [ ] Understand response time expectations for your role
- [ ] Know preferred communication channels for different types of updates

### Documentation Standards
- [ ] Understand where to document work and decisions
- [ ] Know templates and formats for your role's deliverables
- [ ] Understand how to update project artifacts (backlog, risk register, etc.)

### Collaboration Norms
- [ ] Understand team working hours and time zones
- [ ] Know how to schedule meetings and respect team calendar
- [ ] Understand code of conduct and collaboration principles
- [ ] Know how to give and receive feedback constructively

---

## 30-Day Check-in

Schedule a check-in after 30 days to review:
- [ ] Are role responsibilities clear?
- [ ] Are communication channels and expectations understood?
- [ ] Have you successfully contributed to the project?
- [ ] Are there any gaps in knowledge or access that need to be addressed?
- [ ] Do you have what you need to be successful in your role?
